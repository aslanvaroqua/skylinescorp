Installation

Lovely is an HTML template - this means that all domain and hosting should be organised by you. However you should try the Following Guide to Build it on your own Server.

FAQ

  • What do I need to upload to my server?

    You'll need to upload all HTML files along with all directories (css,js,mail,video,img) in the structure that they appear in your download package. That is, all HTML files should reside in the root directory, and all other files should reside inside their associated folder.

  • Where do i get the FTP server information?

    You need to contact your hosting provider and once you have purchased your Hosting the you will get the Hosting information.

  • Where can i get Images?

    All of our images are free and you can get the Unsplash and other Free Image websites. If you really need it then you can get from us and please contact us themewaves at gmail dot com.
